You can pay your installments by logging into your dashboard and navigating to the "View & Pay Installments" section. From there, you can either pay for individual pending installments or pay the full remaining balance at once.

Currently, the system marks payments as PENDING. While there is no automatic penalty, it is important to clear your dues as soon as possible. Your digital bus pass may show an "unpaid" status if you have overdue payments, which could affect your ability to use the service. Please contact the administration for specific policies on late fees.

Your assigned bus details, including the bus number, route, and schedule, are available on your digital bus pass. You can access this by clicking the "View My Bus Pass" button on your dashboard. You can also view a list of all available routes by clicking "View Bus Routes".

For any support-related queries, please contact the transport administration office. Make sure to have your student ID and registered email address ready.
